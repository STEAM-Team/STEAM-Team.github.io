C+STEAM

C+STEAM investigates the ways computer science and technology can create art and beauty, whether through CAD, data visualization, or biohacking. We've held events such as the "mini_me" workshop, in which participants used Kinect scanners, Skanect, and Rhino to capture themselves, which were later 3D printed into mini busts and self portraits.
