UMichigan STEAM Team

Our main theme for our first year as an active organization on campus is science communication. One of our projects will involve collaboration between student researchers in STEM fields and art and design students to create intuitive and creative user interfaces for public platforms discussing discoveries in STEM research across campus for broad audiences.
